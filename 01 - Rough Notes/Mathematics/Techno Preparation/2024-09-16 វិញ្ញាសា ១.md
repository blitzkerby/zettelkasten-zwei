### ប្រធានទី

1. បើ $ax^3+bx^2+x-6$ មាន $x+2$ ជាកត្តាហើយមានសំណល់ 4 ពេលចែកនឹង $x-2$ ។ រកតម្លៃ $a$ និង $b$ រៀងគ្នា។
---

$$
\large{\begin{cases}
	\begin{align}
		ax^{3}+bx^{2}+x-6&=\alpha\left(x+2\right) 
		\\ \\
		 
		\frac{\left(ax^{3}+bx^{2}+x-6\right)}{x-2}&=\beta\left(x-2\right)+4
	\end{align}
\end{cases}}
$$

assume $\large{f(x)=ax^3+bx^2+x-6}$,

$$
\large{\begin{cases}
	\begin{align}
		f(-2) &= 0 \\ \\
		f(2) &= 4
	\end{align}
\end{cases}}
$$

$$

\large{\rightarrow\begin{cases}
	\begin{align}
		-8a + 4x - 2 - 6 &= 0 \\ \\
		8a + 4b + 2 - 6 &= 4
	\end{align}
\end{cases}}
$$

$$
\large{\rightarrow\begin{cases}
	\begin{align}
		-8a + 4b -8 &= 0 \\ \\
		8a + 4b -8 &= 0
	\end{align}
\end{cases}}
$$

$$
\large\begin{align}
	8b - 16 &= 0 \\
	b &= \frac{16}{8} \\
	b &= 2	
\end{align}
$$

$$
\large\begin{align}
	8a + 8 - 8 &= 0 \\
	8a &= 0 \\
	a &= 0 \\
\end{align}
