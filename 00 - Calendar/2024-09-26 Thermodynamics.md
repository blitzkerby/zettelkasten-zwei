#### **Isochoric** : $\Delta V = 0$
---
$$\large{
\begin{matrix} 
	W=0 &&
	Q=n{C_v}{\Delta T} &&
	\Delta U = Q - W 
\end{matrix}
}$$

#### **Isobar** : $\Delta P = 0$
---
$$\large{
\begin{matrix}
	W = P{\Delta V} &&
	Q=n{C_p}{\Delta T} &&
	\Delta U = n{C_v}{\Delta T}
\end{matrix}
}$$

#### **Isothermal** : $\Delta T = 0$
---
$$\large{
\begin{matrix}
	\begin{align}
		W = Q
	\end{align}
	Q=n{C_p}{\Delta T} &&
	\Delta U = n{C_v}{\Delta T}
\end{matrix}
}$$
